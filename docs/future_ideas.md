# Future Ideas

## Removed Meta-Crate Items (to be repurposed)

These custom items were removed from the Olympian meta-progression crates. Their item scripts and mechanics files still exist in the codebase for future use.

### Ceres Hoe
- **Item script**: `ceres_hoe` (in `ceres_items.dsc`)
- **Mechanics**: `ceres_mechanics.dsc` (replants crops on harvest)
- **Previously from**: Ceres Grove crate
- **Flag**: `ceres.item.hoe`

### Mars Sword
- **Item script**: `mars_sword` (in `mars_items.dsc`)
- **Mechanics**: Lifesteal (heals 10% of damage dealt)
- **Previously from**: Mars Arena crate
- **Flag**: `mars.item.sword`

### Vulcan Forge Charm
- **Item script**: `vulcan_forge_charm` (in `vulcan_items.dsc`)
- **Previously from**: Vulcan Crucible crate
- **Flag**: `vulcan.item.charm`
